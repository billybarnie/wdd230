Typography

For the typography portion the author has decided to go with a bold Oswald/ San-Serif font for the headings and sub-heading as a way to bring attention to the content in which is being read by the visitors of the site. The author has also decided to use a regular 400 Bitter/ Serif font for the paragraphs and other smaller texts as a way to make it more readable and easy to pick on. The Bitter/ Serif and the Oswald/ San-Serif have beeen used as a way to both keep a good contrast between the headings and the content.
