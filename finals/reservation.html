Rules and Agreements!

We enjoy that fact that you are ready to rent from us but we have a certain set of rules and regulations set in mind to keep our rentors safe and make sure that they have the most enjoyable experience possible. We have a little bit of a motto behind this that we always say "fun isn't fun without safety!". With that being said we make sure to abide by the rules set in place by our location as well as making sure others abide by it whether it be in the area or out of the area. We also in it let you know the costs of things such as delivery and certain other stipulations. You can see our agreements and rules listed down below for more clarification and remember, happy renting!

  • A $50 security deposit is required to confirm reservations.
  • A valid drivers license is required in hand at the time of rental.
  • No one under the age of 12 years of age are allowed to ride on the scooters.
  • Fuel charge is not included.
  • Unlimited mileage.
  • Liability insurance is included.
  • Damage insurance is available for purchase.
  • Free delivery to Cozumel hotels or Airbnbs for 2+ day rentals.
  • Payment via Google Pay, Apple Pay, cash, credit card, debit card, or venmo.
  • Rentals may be returned to either physical location.
